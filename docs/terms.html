Meridian is a local-first Chrome extension that uses Anthropic's Claude API to summarize pages you visit, answer questions about your browsing, plan research, and surface decisions and manipulation tactics. It is provided as a tool to augment your own judgment — not to replace it.

2. Your Claude API key

Meridian requires you to provide your own Anthropic Claude API key. You are responsible for:

Meridian does not see your key — it is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device and sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's API.
